FAQ

Questions,
answered

What does a personal styling session involve?

Our personal styling sessions begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and goals. We then conduct a wardrobe audit, provide colour and body analysis, and deliver a personalised styling roadmap with shopping recommendations tailored to your budget.

How long does a bespoke garment take to complete?

Bespoke garments typically take 4–8 weeks from initial consultation to final fitting. This includes fabric selection, pattern making, fittings, and finishing. Rush orders may be accommodated at an additional fee depending on availability.

Do you offer virtual styling consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for clients across India and internationally. We simply require you to share photos of your current wardrobe and lifestyle context ahead of the session for the best results.

What is your sustainability approach?

Sustainability is central to everything we do. We source fabrics from certified ethical suppliers, work with local artisans to minimise transport footprint, use zero-waste pattern cutting where possible, and always recommend a "buy less, choose well" philosophy.

Can NOIR dress me for a specific event or occasion?

Absolutely. Our event dressing service covers weddings, galas, red carpet events, and business dinners. We handle outfit selection, alterations, accessories, and on-the-day styling if required.

How do I begin working with NOIR?

Simply reach out via our contact page or email us directly. We'll schedule a complimentary 20-minute discovery call to understand your needs and recommend the right service. From there, we guide you through every step.

Do you work with brands and businesses?

Yes — we collaborate with fashion brands, lifestyle labels, and creative agencies on lookbooks, campaign styling, brand identity alignment, and content creation. Each partnership is scoped individually. Get in touch to discuss your project.
